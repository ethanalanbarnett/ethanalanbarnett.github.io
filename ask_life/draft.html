What is ASK Life?

About Us

ASK Life is a private foundation created with the goal of providing educational resources and support to underprivileged children. Our mission statement, “It takes little contribution to make a lasting effect,” is focused on providing quality education to rural India and other worldwide nations. We believe our children are the change we wish to see in the world and emphasize the importance of providing positive attachment in childhood stages to cultivate a drug-free and educational-based society.

ASK Life was founded by Dr. Kandaswamy Jayaraj and Asha Jayaraj to guide children in human development and constant educational improvement. The foundation and its name are dedicated in remembrance of the late A.S. Kandaswamy as a legacy from his son and daughter-in-law.

We believe education is the primary need for a human being’s betterment and that knowledge is power to change the world. Our vision is to “Live life, not a lifestyle” as we strive to cultivate that in younger generations to lead a peaceful, satisfied life supported by education and learning. With guidance and resources, ASK Life aims to help young adults develop into goal-oriented leaders who will one day make a difference in our society.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

  • "When was ASK ∙ Life founded?"

    ASK ∙ Life was technically founded in 2021, but it wasn't until 2023 that the foundation became active.

  • "Why is it called ASK ∙ Life?"

    ASK are the initials of Dr. Jayaraj's father: something something Kandaswamy. The ASK ∙ Life logo is also the first initial of his fathers name using the Tamil letter எ (making a short E sound in English).

  • "Can I donate?"

    ASK ∙ Life is not looking for donations, but if you would like to help out, you are more than welcome to donate to those causes that ASK ∙ Life is supporting.
